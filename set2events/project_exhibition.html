PROJECT EXHIBITION

“Innovation is the ability to see change as an opportunity-not a threat.”

Technology keeps on advancing day by day & it is necessary for one to know about latest applications in different areas. With innovation as inspiration & technical knowledge as tool, we aim at bringing transformation of ‘TECHNOVATING INDIA’. This can be achieved only when we address real life problems by building & constructing working & non working models.

FUSION-2K18 welcomes projects in various fields of Engineering. Come, be a part of this Endeavour, to use the bright young minds to create the models that can be absorbed in form of development of technology.

General Rules:-

  • Maximum number of students per team is four.
  • The teams must send their abstract of the project along with their individual registration IDs.
  • The completely filled abstract should be sent to becfusion2k18@gmail.com on or before 8th March, 2018.
  • The necessary electrical arrangement required for the model should be sent with the abstract of the project.
  • Selected teams will be intimated through mail.
  • The decision of the judges shall be treated as final & binding on all.

The Project Abstract must be sent as displayed in following format:-

  1. College name: (size 12 in times new roman)
  2. Title of project: (size 14 bold in times new roman)
  3. Team leader: (with contact Number, e-mail id)
  4. Team members: (with contact Number, e-mail id)
  5. Objective: (size 12 in times new roman))
  6. Application: (size 12 in times new roman)
  7. Basic explanation of project: (size 12 in times new roman)
  8. Project requirements: (size 12 in times new roman)
