ROADIES

EXHIBIT THE STRENGTH WITHIN YOU

A roadies is an event collection of management games to test your knowledge, group work and quick decisions to be taken on the spot. The game is designed to throw the worst at you to bring out the best in you. The participants need to show their caliber, the power of persuasion and the ability to handle the pressure. The event is consisting of several rounds both individual and group.

OBJECTIVES:-

  • To provide opportunities to the students at the higher level.
  • The main idea of the event is to help the students work under pressure and take quick decisions on the spot.
  • To encourage students to work in a team to motivate them for corporate world.

RULES AND REGULATIONS:-

  1. The event is open to all the students across all colleges and departments.
  2. Limited registration of 150 (100 in house & 50 out house students).
  3. Individual registration only and teams will be formed by us.
  4. The decision of the organizers of the contest will be final and binding to all the participants.
