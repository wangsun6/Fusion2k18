AD MAD SHOW

BRING OUT THE CREATIVITY WITH YOU

This event tests the creative ability of the students in planning, designing and executing creative advertisements through which they promote their products or services. This event helps the studentsâ€™ to understand and design 4Ps (Product, Price, Place and Promotion) for their products or service and plan strategies to advertise and market them.

OBJECTIVES:-

  • To bring out the creative best out of students in marketing their products.
  • To develop students ability in creating products or services keeping their customers in mind.
  • To impart market survival skills among students.

RULES AND REGULATIONS:-

  1. All students competing in this event must be registered.
  2. Maximum 6 members per team.
  3. Participants must choose any product or service and give brand names, logo, punch lines etc to them which should be innovative, attractive and suitable. The theme for the advertisement should also be decided by the participants.
  4. Participants should get a demo model (just for presentation) in the advertisement.
  5. Student can use audio-visual aids, props (i.e., suitable dresses or any other such supporting materials during presentation).
  6. The ads has to be enacted on live on the stage.
  7. The time limit for each group will be 5 mins and extra 2 mins for interaction with the judges.
