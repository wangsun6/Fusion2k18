BRIDGE BATTLE

This is event to recall the basic knowledge of students with civil aspects and application of civil techniques and simple current events that are going on. The event offers participant to have fun with their basic knowledge and provides platform for every students to express their ability to compete with simple civil aspects.

Fusion-2k18 offers an opportunity to be a part of the national level technical fest.

OBJECTIVES:-

  • To provide an opportunity to compete at the national level technical fest and to exchange ideas and knowledge.
  • To encourage contestants to recall and apply their civil basics.
  • To create awareness and importance of participation and competition.

RULES AND REGULATIONS:-

  1. All students competing in this event must be registered.
  2. Maximum of three members per team.
  3. Participants should be present before 15 minutes.
  4. Decision of the organizing and expert team will be final.
  5. Materials will be provided by host.
