CIRCUIT DESIGNING AND DEBUGGING

Ever been bugged lately and tried to solve it?? Here is your chance to do-so!. This event is related with technology and electronics circuits having lots of fun by providing you great potential. Crack your technical brain to unravel the mystery behind the bugs of the circuit. Do you have it in you to fester all your technical knowledge-How to make rectify the glitch and make it right? If so, then get ready to be bugged!!!

FUSION-2K18 offers an opportunity to be a part of the national level technical fest.

OBJECTIVES:-

  • To get a simple understanding of the basic concepts in electronics for beginners.
  • Design a circuit involving basic electronic components such as resistors, capacitors, inductors, op-amps, transistors and logic gates.
  • To learn techniques for debugging circuits in a systematic manner.

RULES AND REGULATIONS:-

  1. All students competing in this event must be registered.
  2. Individual registration. Later groups will be formed by the jury.
  3. Electronic gadgets are not allowed.
