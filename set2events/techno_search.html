TECHNOSEARCH

It is a event to recall the basic knowledge of students with electrical aspects and application of simple electrical technologies and simple current events that are going on. The event offers participant to have fun with their basic knowledge and provides a platform for every students to express their ability to compete with the simple electrical aspects Fusion -2k18 offers an opportunity to be a part of the national level technical fest.

FUSION-2K18 welcomes projects in various fields of Engineering. Come, be a part of this Endeavour, to use the bright young minds to create the models that can be absorbed in form of development of technology.

OBJECTIVES:-

  • To provide opportunity to compete at the national level event and to exchange ideas and knowledge.
  • To encourage contestants to recall and apply their electrical basics.
  • To create awareness of importance of participation and competition.

RULES AND REGULATIONS:-

  1. All students competing in this event must be registered.
  2. Two members per team.
  3. Participants should be present before 15 minutes.
  4. The participants should not carry mobile phones inside the event hall.)
  5. Decision of the organizing and expert team will be final.
