DNAwesome

DNAwesome comprises 3 rounds.

1st round: Biobridge

Participants have to talk about role of biotechnology in their respective department within 3 minutes

NOTE- preferably oral presentation only

RULES AND REGULATIONS:-

  1. Talk should be completed within 3 minutes

2nd round: Pippete it out

Eligible participants from round 1 will participate in round 2 in this round participants have to pipette solutions accurately according to the given quantity in the respective test tubes within short time

RULES AND REGULATIONS:-

  1. In a single time only one test tube is allowed to filled
  2. Elimination is done on time basis

3rd round: Techquiz

Eligible participants will move on to 3rd round in this round technically based questions are asked

RULES AND REGULATIONS:-

  1. Within 20 secs participants should answer
  2. Game points will be provided based on questions level

#main rules#

  1. Participants should be at the venue before 15min
  2. Jury decision is final.
