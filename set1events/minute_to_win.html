MINUTE TO WIN

“STRONGEST OF ALL WARRIOR'S ARE THESE TWO -TIME AND PATIENCE !!!!!!!”

Minute to win games are very interesting that make the crowd feel thrilled. It emphasise the importance of 60seconds that turns the game into a challenge. This event is all about team bonding and having fun together by getting up and giving it a go. Energy levels soar and colleagues witness the playful sides of one another. Contestants are given some props depending upon the game, the person who completes the task of the game in a minute will be the winner.

Fusion -2K18 offers an opportunity to be the part of the national level technical fest.

OBJECTIVES:-

  • To provide an opportunity to compete at the national level fest.
  • To encourage contestants to tackle the task in a minute.

RULES AND REGULATIONS:-

  1. All the students competing in this event must be registered
  2. Teams will be formed by the jury
  3. The time allotted for each phase of game is 60 seconds
  4. Decision done by the organizing and expert team will be final
