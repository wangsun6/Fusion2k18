SULJAN

A creative problem solving.

Creativity is the most important quality in individuals. Thinking out of the box is the essence of this competition. Success in this competition is mainly based on finding a most distinct solution.

OBJECTIVES:-

  • Analyze a given problem.
  • To find most creative solution.
  • Build a working model.

RULES AND REGULATIONS:-

  1. Participating team should consist of maximum of three members.
  2. The team must register before 09th March 2018.
  3. Presenting only concept without a working model will not be allowed to participate.
  4. Decision of the jury will be final.

NOTE:-

  1. Problem statement will be displayed in below picture.
  2. Participants should be present with their model on the date of competition at the venue.
