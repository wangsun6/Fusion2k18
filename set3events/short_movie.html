SHORT MOVIE

“ In a world of lies and liars , an honest work of art is always an act of social responsibility”

Short movies are a roller-coaster ride that have the tremendous power to entertain and to teach. They can be a powerful tool to bring about effective social change. Intuitive and logical that a well-made film especially one with the compelling narrative and well-crafted out reach plan would serve as a catalyst to change minds, encourage viewers to change entrenched behaviors, and start to inform or reenergize social movements. Change is the only constant and awareness is fundamental to change, this event is to raise awareness about what happening in the society and how to face and overcome that era.Entries in this competition can touch on this social awareness and might expedite require change.

FUSION 2K18 offers an opportunity to be the part of the national level technical fest.

RULES AND REGULATIONS:-

  1. The short movie must be based on social awareness.
  2. The total runtime of the short movie, including opening and closing credits, should strictly be a minimum 5 minutes and a maximum of 7 minutes.
  3. Judges decision will be the final binding.
