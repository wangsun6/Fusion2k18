SPEAK FOR INDIA

“RAISE YOUR VOICE FOR INDIA, BATTELE FIELD IS ON,COME CHARGED……..!!!!!”

It is an event to encourage young,dynamic and enthusiastic minds across the nation to raise their voice on pertinent issues and play the role of change maker in society. The event offers participant a podium to explore their awareness, insight and perspective on contemporary topics.

Fusion -2K18 offers an opportunity to be a part of the national level technical fest.

OBJECTIVES:-

  • To provide an opportunity to compete at the national level event and to exchange ideas and knowledge.
  • To encourage contestants to hone their public speaking skills.
  • To create awareness in youths about issues in India.

RULES AND REGULATIONS:-

  1. All students competing in this event must be registered.
  2. The speechcan either be in English or Kannada.
  3. The maximum time allotted is 5(4+1) minutes.
  4. Decision of the organizing and expert team will be final.

Topic: Building Patriotism in Indian Youth's
