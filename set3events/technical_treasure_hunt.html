TECHNICAL TREASURE HUNT

The main aim of the event “TECHNICAL TREASURE HUNT” is to enhance memory capacity and their ability to work as a team.

Fusion-2k18 offers an opportunity to be a part of the national level technical fest.

Schedule: 11/03/2018 @10 AM

Venue: CSE department

OBJECTIVES:-

  • To provide an opportunity to compete at the national level event and to exchange ideas and knowledge.
  • To encourage contestants to improve their technical skills, test the stamina and patience of students.

RULES AND REGULATIONS:-

  1. All students competing in this event must be registered and should carry their college ID cards.
  2. Decision of the organizing and expert team will be final.
  3. Teams will be formed by us.
  4. Requirements for the event will be provided by the information desk.
  5. No team members should posses any kind of internet, electronic gadgets unless instructed to do so.
  6. The clue has to be found in a particular order. The team cannot skip a clue. Skipping a clue leads to disqualification of the team.
  7. An entire team must stay together. Do not split up to find different clues, it is considered as violation and team will be disqualified.
  8. All the means used by the teams should be fair, in case of any violation of the rules or any use of unfair means, teams will be disqualified from the contest.
  9. For more info contact Student Coordinators, their details are furnished below.
