A vision for life

It is an event conducted to have learners describe and discuss a variety of entrepreneurial skills and competencies that are necessary for a successful entrepreneur.

Fusion-2K18 offers an opportunity to be a part of the national level technical fest.

OBJECTIVES:-

  1. To provide an opportunity to enter into an exciting, unpredictable and happening world of operation in the market.
  2. Play, experience and understand the importance of time and effective management through a stimulated environment.

However you need not be an operations wizard to participate in this event. All you need to do is to put your thinking cop on.

SUB-ROUNDS:-

  1. BRAIN-DRILL: This round helps to accelerate logic, encourage imagination and exercises your brain.
  2. DEVIL FOLLOWS: This is the innovative management round which would give students a lesson how to work in team and create team building attitude amongst them.
  3. KARYANEETI: The objective of this round is to see how students can think in the different way towards a particular solution and handle it wisely.
  4. 50 KA FUNDA: This round ensures that the young generation has the skills required to capitalize on todayâ€™s opportunities.
  5. CAMPUS MANIA: The participants get grilled with questions and different selection rounds procedure that they would normally undergo during recruitment process.
