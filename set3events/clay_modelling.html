CLAY MODELING

This event is to explore creative minds in people across the nation to showcase their designing and modelling skills. Through this event a participant gets an opportunity to show their extreme designing and modelling skills on the topic.

Fusion-2k18 provides an opportunity to a part of the national level technical fest.

OBJECTIVES:-

  • To provide an opportunity to compete at the national level event and to expose their immense knowledge and ideas.
  • To encourage participants in developing their designing and modelling skills.

RULES AND REGULATIONS:-

  1. All the participants must be registered to the event.
  2. Time duration: 2 hours.
  3. Model should be done on basis of the topic provided on the spot by the jury.
  4. Team of 2 members can take participate.
  5. Clay will be provided and other accessories should be brought by participants.
  6. Electronics gadgets are prohibited during the event.
